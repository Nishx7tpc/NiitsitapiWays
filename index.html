Who are Blackfoot People?

Blackfoot people are a group of First Nations people that have lived in North America for thousands of years.
The Blackfoot people call themselves Niitsitapi which translates to "The Real People" in English, or Siksikaitsitapi which translates to Blackfoot People.

Blackfoot Territory

The Blackfoot people travelled to follow their food sources within their traditional territory. They also, travelled to trade and develop relationships with other tribal nations. (medicines blankets, baskets, hides).


The Blackfoot Territories

North
The North Saskatchewan River.
East
The Great Sandhills.
South
The Yellowstone River.
West
The Rocky Mountains.
