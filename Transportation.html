Transportation

Dog Days

Before settlers arrived to North America and introduced the horse,
Blackfoot people had help from the wolves to carry their belongings. Wolves back then acted just like the dogs we own today.

Horses

Around the 15-1600's era, The Spaniards brought horses over and introduced them to the Shoshonee Tribe.
The Shoshonee tribe travelled North and introduced the Blackfoot people to this new creature.
